What is Jolt?

Jolt is a social networking app inspired by 2007 Twitter. Share what you're doing in 140 characters or less!

Getting Started

New users: Click "Sign Up" to create your account. You'll receive two keys:

  • Private key (nsec): Keep this secret! It's like your password. Never share it.
  • Public key (npub): Share this to let people find you.

Features

  • Post sparks: Share updates up to 140 characters
  • Reply: Respond to others' sparks
  • Favorite: Show appreciation with a star
  • View profiles: Click any username or avatar
  • Mentions: See when others mention you

What's Nostr?

Jolt is built on Nostr, a decentralized protocol. This means:

  • Your data isn't stored on our servers - it's distributed across the network
  • You can use your Jolt account with other Nostr apps (Damus, Primal, etc.)
  • No company controls your account - you own your identity

Privacy & Safety

  • Your private key (nsec) is your identity - keep it safe!
  • We filter crypto spam and NSFW content by default
  • Follower counts are hidden for your mental health

Tips

  • Save your private key somewhere safe - we can't recover it if lost
  • Check the "Show only Jolt posts" filter to see just our community
  • Click on sparks to see replies
  • Update your profile picture and bio in Profile settings
